About Us

Higgy Piggy Productions is an industry leading design and engineering team specialsiing in bringing our client’s ideas and inventions right through from an idea on paper to a finished product ready for international distribution. Specifically we have established great links and work very closely with the plastic and metal fabrication industry and we look forward to working with new clients in the future.

We are a very passionate team of Design, CAD and Software Engineers from the UK and we’re very proud to announce our new videography sector to the team, offering a full package to the client with the added benefit of a promotional video.
